Our Teacher Khawja Tahir Mehmood

Intro

He is Lecturer Department of Computer Science Federal Urdu University of Arts, Science & Technology. Islamabad.He has completed his MS(CS) Degree in 2004 from M.A.J.U Islamabad. Over the period of 20 years in Teaching, he has also served in various administrative positions as Head of Department (BS), In charge Final Year Project, Director Student Affairs, In charge Sports, Member University Disciplinary Committee, Program Coordinator, Event Organizer etc.. During his career he had served in various reputable Organizations i.e Punjab Group of Colleges (PGC), University of Central Punjab (UCP). He joined FUUAST Islamabad as permanent faculty member in 2009 as Lecturer (Computer Science) & taught courses li.e. Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Software Project Management (SPM), Software Engineering, Object Oriented Software Engineering & Final Year Project at Undergraduate & Graduate level. He is also having a Youtube channel i.e. KHAWAJA SCIENCE ACADEMY which is been successfully created in 2020 (covid time) for the students. He has also supervised many Final Year Research Projects for FUUAST Islamabad i.e Medical Billing System, Graduate Research Management System, Transport Management System, Diary Management System, Account Management System. He has been originator of Final Year Project (FYP Course) in 2014. He has also published research article in IEEE. His area of Research is in Human Computer Interaction, Usability Evaluation, Mobile Usability, Software Engineering, Databases.

Course Objective

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Understand the principles and theories of human-computer interaction and usability design.
  • Evaluate and design user interfaces for optimal user experience.
  • Apply cognitive psychology concepts to improve interface interactions.
  • Utilize tools and techniques to prototype and test user interfaces effectively.
  • Collaborate in teams to solve complex interaction design problems.
